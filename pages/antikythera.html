December 2013 Role : UX & UI Design

Antikythera

From the brief we had to think a web solution according to a specific topic . Our topic was Masterpiece and we decided to tell the story of an old mecanism made by Archimède in -87 BC.

My role

On this project I worked on the UI / UX design. I wanted to create an immersive experience in fullscreen with a simple naviguation and a minimalist User Interface.

Immerse yourself into the story

To create a true experience we incorporate visual and sonor effects. Thanks to the timeline you can navigate between each section and trace the story of this unique and misunderstood object in this time.

See the mecanism

To better understand the mechanism we wanted to have a part of the website dedicated to it. In this section you can see the whole mecanism animated with each gear rotating.

Share what you've learned

Once your trips ends share your experience on the social networks for other people learn more about it . Even today we can’t measure the impact of this piece if it hadn’t been lost.
