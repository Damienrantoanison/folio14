May 2014 Role : Webdesign & Illustrator

Simpson Museum

From the brief we had to think a web solution according to a specific topic . Our topic was Masterpiece and we decided to tell the story of an old mecanism made by Archimède in -87 BC.

My role

On this project I worked on the UI / UX design. I wanted to create an immersive experience in fullscreen with a simple naviguation and a minimalist User Interface.

We separated the data obtained in 3 sections, the first part is about the ratings and viewers, the second on the characters and a last part on recurring funny facts in simpson serie.

Characters Illustrations

To better understand the mechanism we wanted to have a part of the website dedicated to it. In this section you can see the whole mecanism animated with each gear rotating.

Robin Payot, Emmanuelle Misson, Marion Livet, Martin Debray and Damien Lecolinet

Mars 2014 Role : Webdesign & Motion Design

Handly

Handly is a concept design it has not been subsequently developed. The idea was to create an application that can bring people together and they can exchange expertise.

My role

I worked on webdesign, the logo, some icons and a motion design. I wanted to create a simple service, friendly and intuitive that can be adapted to many users.

Share your skills

Find the right person who can help you through the area of ​​expertise. Send a request if you want to find an arrangement with that person. If your request is accepted it's time to get in touch with her.

The Logo

I first draw various ideas and concepts on my sketchbook and then i go to the vector. I try to use simple shapes to make a hand and grid the logo to make it more consistent and recognizable.

The Icons

I also made several icons for this project and each represents the area of expertise.

Motion Design

During a week focused on motion design we had to design a motion of fifteen seconds explaining one of our project. I choose to do mine on Handly and simply explain the purpose of the app.

December 2013 Role : UX & UI Design

Antikythera

From the brief we had to think a web solution according to a specific topic . Our topic was Masterpiece and we decided to tell the story of an old mecanism made by Archimède in -87 BC.

My role

On this project I worked on the UI / UX design. I wanted to create an immersive experience in fullscreen with a simple naviguation and a minimalist User Interface.

Immerse yourself into the story

To create a true experience we incorporate visual and sonor effects. Thanks to the timeline you can navigate between each section and trace the story of this unique and misunderstood object in this time.

See the mecanism

To better understand the mechanism we wanted to have a part of the website dedicated to it. In this section you can see the whole mecanism animated with each gear rotating.

Share what you've learned

Once your trips ends share your experience on the social networks for other people learn more about it . Even today we can’t measure the impact of this piece if it hadn’t been lost.

Antoine Vidal, Dorian Camilleri, Dany Graells, Douglas Andrès and Mathieu Menut

October 2013 Role : Front-developer

Dreamcatcher

During two weeks centered on the WordPress development we had to create a VDM like. We think about creating a plateform where everyone can come and post his dream.

My role

On this project I was involved in developing the template for the single view of a dream posted.

Post your weirdest dream

Your dream last night was really weird. Post it in one of the eight categories and see if users think it's a dream or a nightmare. Maybe your dream will rise in the top best dreams or nightmares.

Record your dreams orally

Thanks to an awesome plugin we have obtained a phone number that people can call and record their dreams orally. Then we can listen the recorded audio track.
